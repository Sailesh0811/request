Once upon a time, long long ago

In an "alternate" universe, There once lived a family so powerful that they tamed dragons and ruled over a huge empire. It was a golden age, they had a mighty army whose marching echoed through the mountains.They conquered many smaller kingdoms, and many noble families swore fealty to the king.They ruled that land with no one to oppose them.

One fine day

The king's most trusted commander betrayed him and killed the entire royal family, sparing only the princess. Who was married off to a "native" warlord from a far off land. The warlord was greatly feared in those regions. He controlled hordes of horses and blood-thirsty warriors. He had a strong influence over the smaller kingdoms in the surrounding regions.

On the day of marriage

Many rulers from the nearby kingdoms came to offer their wishes and gave them gifts. The gifts varied from exotic animals and rare gemstones to chests of gold. Among them the most peculiar ones were the three dragon eggs which were retrieved from the deep pockets of the world.

Meanwhile...

The Commander's family took control of the kingdom, and exploited its riches to satisfy their greed for power and wealth.The princess catches wind about the tyranny and turmoil of her kingdom and decides to take back what's rightfully hers.

So now...

The princess knows her husband's army won't be sufficient enough to defeat the commander's army and take over her kingdom.So, she must now forge ties between "unseeming" allies and raise an army strong enough to defeat the Commander and his family.

The Quest..

Embark on a quest with the princess and help her overcome the obstacles that come in her way.And help her reclaim her "THRONE"
